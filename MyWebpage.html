About us:

Common Grounds Coffee House & Desserts is just that, an awesome coffee shop serving delicious desserts. Our flavor selections for your coffee are endless with not only classics like hazelnut and vanilla but also specials like pomegranate and lavender. Besides we have unique decor, Common Grounds is known for its huge selection of one-of-a-kind coffee mugs for sale. We have indoor and outdoor seating.

HOT COFFEE:

Homemade Chai Latte
- an aromatic beverage that blends black tea with various herbs and spices. The most common spices used in chai are cinnamon, cardamom, ginger, clove, black pepper, and star anise.

Almond Latte
- A delicious homemade latte with espresso, milk, and almond simple syrup. Filled with nutty flavor and so easy to make!

Honey Latte
- A honey latte is a coffee drink that combines honey and milk.

Charleston Latte
- Delightfully aromatic and well-balanced with subtle notes of chocolate and caramel.

Macchiato
- an espresso coffee drink, topped with a small amount of foamed or steamed milk to allow the taste of the espresso to still shine through

Vietnamese Coffee
- strong and bold with very deep flavors and complex flavor notes that can be simultaneously sweet, refreshing, savory, and earthy

White Raspberry Latte
- Double espresso with milk, Ghirardelli white chocolate sauce and raspberry syrup

Black and Coke
- contain less caffeine than a regular cup of coffee but more than a regular can of Coke

Gunpowder Pearl Green Tea
- a rich, roasted, slightly smoky aroma with a sweet vegetal flavor and a satisfying astringency

Classic Latte
- made with a single or double shot of espresso (1/3 of your drink) and 2/3 of your drink is steamed milk with a small layer (around 1 cm) of frothed milk.
